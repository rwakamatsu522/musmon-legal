FAQ

  • Restore a purchase: open the subscription screen and tap "Restore".
  • Cancel a subscription: iPhone Settings → your name (Apple ID) → Subscriptions.
  • Report an inappropriate user: open the user's profile and tap the report icon (top right).
  • Transfer or recover your data: in-app Settings → Data transfer (recover by email).
  • Change the language: in-app Settings → Language.
  • Delete your account or data: in-app Settings → Delete account (you can do this yourself anytime).
